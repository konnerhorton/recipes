# Cashew Sweet Cream

A (sort of) whipped cream substitute

## Ingredients

- 1 cup cashews raw
- 2 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 tbsp sweetener (honey, sugar, syrup)
- salt

## Preparation

1. Soak cashews overnight.
2. Drain cashews and blend with other ingredients.
3. Sweeten and salt to taste.
