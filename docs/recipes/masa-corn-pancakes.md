# Masa corn pancakes

## Ingredients

- 2 ea eggs
- 0.5 cup masa flour
- 1 cup water
- 0.5 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp oil
- 2 tbsp sugar

## Preparation

1. Crack eggs in bowl, add water, salt, and sugar
2. Wisk / beat the mixture well
3. Add masa and mix well
4. Let sit for ~30 minutes
5. Make pancakes!
