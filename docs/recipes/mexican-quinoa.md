# Mexican Quinoa

## Ingredients

- 1 cup quinoa (uncooked)
- 1.5 cup black beans (cooked)
- 14 oz can diced tomatoes
- 3 bell peppers
- 4 cloves garlic
- 1 ea small onion
- oil
- 1 cup broth
- chili powder
- cumin powder

### Garnish

- avocado
- lime
- cilantro

## Preparation

1. Cook quinoa and black beans (if needed).
1. Add all ingredients (not the garnish) to a pot, bring to boil, and simmer 20 minutes.
