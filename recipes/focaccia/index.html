Focaccia

Ingredients

  • 500 gram all-purpose flour
  • 10 gram kosher salt
  • 4 gram instant dry yeast
  • 400 gram room temperature water
  • 68 gram extra-virgin olive oil
  • Coarse sea salt

Preparation

Make the Dough

  1. Combine flour, kosher salt, and yeast in bowl.
  2. Mix in water and stir until no dry flour remains.
  3. Add 20 grams of olive oil, use hands to work it into the dough.
  4. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and let rest at room temperature for 1 hour.
  5. Remove plastic wrap, using a greased scraper fold dough over itself (lift and fold one edge toward middle).
  6. Rotate 45 degrees and repeat (keep rotating / repeating for 6 total times).
  7. Cover and let rest for 15 minutes.
  8. Repeat steps 5 and 6 again.
  9. Cover bowl tightly with plastic wrap and store in refrigerator for 18 to 72 hours.

Bake

  1. Remove dough from the refrigerator and let sit at room temperature for 10 minutes.
  2. Sprinkle with flour then transfer to a floured working surface.
  3. Tuck the dough underneath intself until it is a tight ball.
  4. Pour 27 grams of olive oil into 10 or 12 inch skillet (or other baking pan), spread over entire surface (including rim).
  5. Place dough in pan and coat in oil (seam side down).
  6. Spread the dough out to flatten, don't worry if the dough does not reach the edges.
  7. Cover tightly and let sit for 1.5 to 2 hours (it will become soft and puffy).
  8. One hour before baking, preheat oven to 500 deg F and place a baking stone or steal on lower-middle position rack.
  9. After the dough is puffy, use oiled fingers to press the dough to the edges of the pan, you may need to lift the dough up in places to allow air bubbles to escape.
  10. Create small dimples in the dough surface with your fingers and drizzle remaining oil (20 grams) over the dough.
  11. Sprinkle with sea salt.
  12. Place skillet / pan on top of baking steel / stone.
  13. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, rotate the pan 180 degrees half-way through the baking process.
  14. Let bread rest for 5 minutes after removal from oven, then brush with a little more oil.
  15. Let cool for 10 more minutes before it's ready.
