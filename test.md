# Broccoli Salad

## Ingredients
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 2 heads broccoli
- 8 slices bacon
- 1 cup red onion
- 0.5 cup raisins
- 0.5 cup pumpkin seeds

## Preparation

Could use sunflower (or other) seeds instead of pumpkin.

### Instructions

1. Mix 1 cup mayonnaise and 2 tbsp apple cider vinegar in a small bowl, then set aside.

2. Chop 2 heads broccoli into small pieces (small bites) and place in a large bowl.

3. Chop or crumble 8 slices bacon into small bits.

4. Add the bacon, 1 cup red onion, 0.5 cup raisins, and 0.5 cup pumpkin seeds to the broccoli.

5. Mix in the salad dressing.
